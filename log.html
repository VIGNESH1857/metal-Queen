Welcome to Metal Queen

Our company, Metal Queen, is a renowned and trusted entity involved in manufacturing and supplying a wide range of high-quality products to our esteemed customers. Established in the year 1999 as a Sole Proprietorship (Individual) based company in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, we have built a reputation for excellence and reliability.

Our offered range of services includes Metal Staircase, Window Grill, Safety Door Grill, Stainless Steel Balcony Grill, Handrail Fabrication Service, Mezzanine Floor, Safety Doors, Stainless Steel Handrail, Compound Wall Grill, Residential Gate, and many more. Additionally, we provide specialized services such as Stainless Steel Tank Fabrication Service and Handrail Fabrication Service.

With years of experience and dedication, Metal Queen has become the most trusted company in our industry, consistently delivering superior quality and exceptional service to our customers.
